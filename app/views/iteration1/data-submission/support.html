Contact support

If you can’t find what you need in the guidance or you’re experiencing an issue with the portal, you can contact the NDSP Service Desk.
Service Desk email address: ssd.nationalservicedesk@nhs.net

When contacting support, please include:

  • Your organisation
  • The data collection you’re working with
  • A brief description of the issue

Please do not include any personal, confidential, or patient-identifiable information in your message. This will help us respond to your request as quickly as possible.
