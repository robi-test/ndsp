You can request permissions to submit data and/or act as an administrator on behalf of an organisation. This can be your own organisation or another, such as when your organisation submits data on behalf of a different provider. You’ll be directed to the appropriate permission request process based on the role you request: Submitter or Local administrator.

Roles description

Submitter

You’ll be able to submit data on behalf of your organisation for this collection. If a local administrator is already assigned for your organisation and this collection, your request will be reviewed and approved by them. If there is no local administrator, your request will need to be approved by your organisation’s Information Governance (IG) lead and will then be processed by the service desk.

Local administrator

You’ll be responsible for managing user permissions for this collection within your organisation. This includes submitter permissions, allowing you to submit data. Your request must be approved by your organisation’s Information Governance (IG) lead and will be processed by the service desk.

What you’ll need to provide

If you need IG approval, you’ll also be asked to provide the name and contact details of your organisation’s Information Governance (IG) lead. All requests require you to confirm the organisation’s ODS code.

If you don’t know the organisation’s ODS code, you can find it on ODS portal.
