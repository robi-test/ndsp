What do these roles mean?

Before requesting permissions, you should have agreed within your team which role matches your responsibilities. Your role determines what you can see and do in the portal, and which approval route your request follows.
Read the guidance about roles and permissions to understand which role is appropriate for your responsibilities.

Submitter

Submitters can upload and manage data submissions for a specific organisation and collection. This role may also allow access to submission history, validation feedback and related submission activity.

Local Administrator

Local Administrators manage user access for their organisation. They can review and manage permission requests and help ensure the right users have access to the correct collections and roles.

Monitoring role

Monitoring users can view submission and performance information for organisations assigned to their overseer group. This role supports oversight activities by allowing users to monitor submission progress, identify organisations that have not submitted data, review validation errors and warnings, and track data quality and performance trends.
Monitoring users cannot submit data, manage user permissions, approve access requests, or change collection configuration settings.
