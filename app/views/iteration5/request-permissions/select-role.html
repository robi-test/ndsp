What do these roles mean?

Before requesting access, you should already have agreed within your team which role or roles you need. Different roles provide different levels of access and may follow different approval routes.
Read the guidance to understand which role is appropriate for your responsibilities.

[Read guidance about NDSP roles and permissions]

Submitter

Submitters can upload and manage data submissions for a specific organisation and collection. This role may also allow access to submission history, validation feedback and related submission activity.

Local Administrator

Local Administrators manage user access for their organisation. They can review and manage permission requests and help ensure the right users have access to the correct collections and roles.

Monitoring role

Monitoring users can view submission and performance information to support organisations with their submissions. This may include monitoring deadlines, submission progress, validation outcomes and performance for organisations within an overseer group, depending on the permissions granted.
