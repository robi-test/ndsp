What happens next?

Your request will now be reviewed in two steps:

  • It will first be sent to your organisation’s Information Governance (IG) manager for approval.
  • Once approved by the IG manager, it will be reviewed by the service desk, who will grant your permissions.

You’ll receive email notifications at each step or if any additional information is needed.

If you do not receive an update within 5 working days, please contact our support team.

Delays can sometimes occur because:
  • The request is awaiting review by the approver (selected IG manager) and there are high volumes of requests.
  • The approver's contact details are no longer up to date on the ODS portal.
  • The approver is unavailable or no longer active in their role.
